Mnemos

Recognize the people in your home, on your terms.

Mnemos is a self-hosted facial recognition engine. Drop in a snapshot, get back the people you know — and the faces you don't. Works on its own, or integrate it with anything that speaks HTTP (Home Assistant included).

CPU-only inference with InsightFace — no GPU required.
pgvector embeddings in a private Postgres store.
Encrypted session cookies, Argon2id password hashing.
